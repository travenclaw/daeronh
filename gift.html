Miraculum Parvum

Veritas Ex Me

By Daeron Hale • 30 Viae

Always loving you, no matter what.

You are the most beautiful gift in my life.

You are the love of my life.

Your smile is something so beautiful.

Your laughter is my happiness.

You are the most handsome person in my life.

You are my world.

You are my home.

You are my place of rest.

Your eyes are where I want to lose myself again and again.

Your embrace makes the world feel at peace.

With you, everything feels possible.

Your name is the most frequent prayer I speak in my heart.

You make everything feel more alive.

Without you, my days lose their color.

You are the only reason I believe in love again.

I always want to be the reason you smile.

You are the song I never get tired of listening to.

My love for you grows even in the smallest things you don't notice.

You came like light in my darkest night.

In my silence, it's you I always mention.

Everything about you makes me fall in love, again and again.

You make everything simple feel special.

If I had to choose again, I would still choose you.

You are the miracle I silently hoped for.

Loving you is the most natural thing I've ever done.

I don't need the world if I'm already with you.

My heart always returns to you, no matter how far I go.

You are not only my love, but also my best friend and my place to return to.

My love for you will never go anywhere— it will stay, even when I can't.
